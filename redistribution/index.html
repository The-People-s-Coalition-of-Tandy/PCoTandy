What is Negative Trading Theory?

Negative trading was a Wheatport exploit in which an individual could "give" a negative amount of wheat to another individual. For a brief period of time this enabled wheat traders to steal wheat from each other.

Post-Scarcity Negative Wheat Trade

A negative trade could be of an arbitrarily high amount, stealing more wheat than the victim had. In practice, traders were stealing from strangers. The People's Coalition of Tandy noted that if a fake account was created, anybody could generate limitless wheat from the ownerless target. This method would create limitless wheat with no harm or deception, a core goal of the Coalition. Negative Trading is no longer possible, but PCoTandy is releasing this memo to inspire and educate.
